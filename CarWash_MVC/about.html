about us

Conveniently located at (1166 Victoria St N, Kitchener, ON N2B 3C9), to provide quality car wash services to Kitchener and its surrounding areas. We are open every day, weather permitting, except major holidays. Friendly and customer focused service is our earned reputation. Our goal at Kitchener Car Wash is to provide the best service to each and every vehicle, whether a basic wash or a supreme detail package, we treat every vehicle with care as if it was our own. We pride ourselves in delivering and maintaining the absolute best service, quality work, and safety for your vehicle and to our loyal customers! If your vehicle needs exterior and interior cleaning, bring it to Kitchener Car Wash for unparalleled car wash services. Choose from a variety of car wash packages which include services such as washing, polishing, clear coat shields, and tire cleaning among others. If your vehicle truly needs to be treated, we offer hand waxing and high speed buffing to remove scratches and keep your vehicle looking good for some time to come. Please come by and give us the opportunity to share that reputation with you while we give your car a quality car wash.
